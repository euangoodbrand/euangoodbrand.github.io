AI Movie Review Interpreter

Using Naive-Bayes Multinomial-Classification to classify movie review as good bad or in-between.

  • GitHub Link
  • |
  • Python

Software built using Python to analyze Movie Reviews and determine the writers overall opinion of the movie using AI.

Why is this better than a star rating? Star rating tell you the overall opinion of the review but this method can classify objects within the review and let you know exactly what part of the movie was good or bad. Was the sound good or bad? Were the visual effects good or bad?
