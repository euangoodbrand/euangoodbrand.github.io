My Skills

Throughout my journey in tech, I've cultivated a diverse skill set, constantly adapting myself. Below are some areas I focus.

Software Engineering

I've dedicated myself to crafting software solutions that address real-world problems. Whether it's a sleek mobile app or a robust OS, my aim is to bridge people's needs with technical innovation.

Cloud Computing

I utilise the power of the cloud to provide scalable and efficient solutions. From utilizing SaaS platforms to leveraging PaaS, I've got experience in AWS and Google Cloud.

Creative Design

My passion lies in combining aesthetics with utility. Through disciplines like UI/UX and graphic design, I bring things to life, ensuring audiences enjoy interacting with them in the digital realm.

Internet of Things (IoT)

With IoT, I've explored embedding connectivity into everyday objects. My work in this space is all about connecting the tangible and digital worlds. I enjoy tinkering with parts to build cool stuff such as a card shuffling machine I built recently.

Graphics and Procedural Modeling

Graphics are more than just visuals for me; they're a medium of expression. I'm particularly fond of procedural modeling, using algorithms to generate intricate and diverse 3D designs, bringing things to life.

Artificial Intelligence

I've always been captivated by the potential of AI. Through crafting algorithms that emulate human intelligence, I've contributed to projects that span from chess bots, computer vision, and I am currently doing my MSc on the topic.
